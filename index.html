About EvanCoin

EvanCoin is named after its creator, Evan Andrewson. The name "Evan" has ancient Welsh origins, derived from "Iefan," meaning "God is gracious." Just as its namesake suggests, EvanCoin aims to bring grace and efficiency to the world of digital transactions.
